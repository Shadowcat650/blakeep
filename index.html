Drive Experience Log

Master your driving skills and ace your DMV requirements with Drive Experience Log, the ultimate app designed for student drivers! Whether you're learning to drive or are supervising a new driver, Drive Experience Log simplifies tracking driving hours with precision and ease. Say goodbye to messy paper logs and hello to a streamlined, digital solution.
Key Features:
- DMV-Ready PDF Export: Generate professional PDF logs to submit directly to the DMV, showcasing your driving progress.
- Multi-User Drive Logs: Track driving hours for multiple learners in one app—perfect for families or instructors.
- Smart Day/Night Detection: Automatically detects sunrise and sunset times to split drives into day and night segments, ensuring accurate hour tracking.
- Progress Tracking: Set personalized daytime and nighttime hour goals and watch your progress grow.
- CSV Import/Export: Easily transfer your logs between devices or back them up with CSV file support.
- Manual Drive Entry: Log drives manually with ease, perfect for transferring from other logs or adding untimed sessions.
- iCloud Sync: Keep your driving data safe and accessible across all your devices with seamless cloud integration.
- Quick Start/Stop Shortcuts: Log drives effortlessly with shortcuts to start and end sessions.
- Notes & Tags: Add notes and tags to organize drives by conditions, routes, or milestones.
Drive Experience Log takes the hassle out of logging your supervised driving hours, so you can focus on what matters—becoming a confident, skilled driver. Download now and hit the road to success!

Crowns Score Keeper

Elevate your Five Crowns card game experience with Crowns Score Keeper, the ultimate companion app for seamless score tracking and game management!
- Multiple Ongoing Games: Manage several games at once with ease, perfect for game nights or tournaments.
- Game History: Review past games to relive epic moments or settle friendly disputes.
- Player Statistics: Track individual player performance with detailed stats to see who’s the true Five Crowns champion.
- Unlimited Players: Bring everyone to the party—add as many players as you’d like!
- Score Calculation: Automatically calculate scores for quick, error-free results.
- Dealer Tracking: Stay on top of who’s dealing each round for a smooth flow.
- iCloud Sync: Keep your games and scores synced across devices, so you’re always ready to play.
Whether you're a casual player or a Five Crowns fanatic, Crowns Score Keeper makes tracking scores simple, fun, and reliable. Download now and crown your game nights with precision!
